The **Settings** page shows your account and usage information.

## Plan and balances

The page shows:

- current plan,
- remaining transcription time,
- remaining TTS credits.

If you have a Pro subscription, you can open subscription management from this page.

## Buy more transcription time

Click **Buy time**, choose a package, and complete payment. Purchased transcription time is added to your balance.

## Buy TTS credits

Click **Buy credits**, choose an amount, and complete payment. TTS credits are used for text-to-speech generation.

## Telegram

You can connect the Telegram bot and send voice messages for automatic transcription. Click **Connect Telegram** to link your account.

## Email notifications

You can enable or disable emails that notify you when a transcript is ready.

## Support

For help, contact [support@xosum.am](mailto:support@xosum.am).
