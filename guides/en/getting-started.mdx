Xosum.am is a set of tools for working with Armenian speech and audio content. Most user workflows start in the web app at [app.xosum.am](https://app.xosum.am).

<Info>
  Use Xosum.am for meetings, interviews, lectures, voice notes, support calls, sales calls, and Armenian audio content workflows.
</Info>

## 1. Sign in

Sign in with your Google account. On first login, the app may show an onboarding modal asking how you plan to use Xosum.am.

## 2. Choose a workflow

- **Speech to text** — record or upload audio and receive a transcript.
- **Text to speech** — generate Armenian speech from written text.
- **History** — find and manage previous recordings.
- **AI Analysis** — Business-plan workflow for QA checklists and call evaluation.
- **API** — Business-plan workflow for automated integrations.
- **Reports** — view usage, duration, storage, and estimated time saved.

## 3. Understand credits

Speech-to-text usage is based on audio duration. Text-to-speech usage is based on TTS credits. You can see both balances in **Settings**.

## 4. Create your first result

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Speech to text">
    This is the fastest way to create your first transcript.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload or record audio">
    Use an existing file for meetings and calls, or record directly in the browser for quick notes.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review the transcript">
    After upload, the app opens the recording details page when processing starts.
  </Step>
</Steps>
