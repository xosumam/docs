AI Agents are designed for businesses that want to automate parts of inbound or outbound phone workflows.

<Info>
  Treat this feature as pilot or limited-access unless it is enabled for your account.
</Info>

<Tip>
  AI Agents are most relevant for businesses that need first-line call handling, appointment collection, lead qualification, scripted outbound calls, or after-hours phone coverage.
</Tip>

## What agents can do

- answer inbound calls,
- place outbound calls,
- follow behavior rules you define,
- use a knowledge base,
- connect to a phone number on your account,
- keep call history.

## Main setup fields

When creating an AI agent, you configure:

- name,
- greeting,
- language,
- voice,
- phone number,
- behavior instructions,
- knowledge base.

## Conversation history

The agent page shows completed conversations, duration, recordings, and transcripts.
