The **Text to speech** page lets you turn written Armenian text into generated audio.

<Info>
  Use this for Armenian voiceovers, social video audio, announcements, short learning content, demos, and voice samples.
</Info>

## Generate speech

<Steps>
  <Step title="Write or paste text">
    Short, clear sentences usually sound more natural.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose voice and tone">
    Tone changes the delivery style of the same text.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Generate and listen">
    When the audio is ready, you can play it or download it.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Voices and tone

The app includes multiple voice options with different characteristics. Tone controls help the same text sound like a newsreader, an energetic presenter, a concerned speaker, or Western Armenian.

## Western Armenian

Choose the Western Armenian tone when you want Western Armenian pronunciation. For best results, write the text in Western Armenian and keep sentences reasonably short.

## Limits and credits

Free and paid plans can have different character limits and TTS credit amounts. Remaining TTS credits are visible in **Settings**.

## Errors

<Warning>
  If generation fails, shorten the text, simplify the content, or try another tone. Some content may not pass safety filters.
</Warning>
