The **History** page is the main list of your transcriptions.

## History table

The table shows recording title, source, status, duration, and creation time. Click a row to open the recording details page.

<Tip>
  History is especially useful when your team has many meetings or calls and needs to find recordings by source, date, customer, operator, or metadata.
</Tip>

## Search and filters

You can filter by:

- source: upload, browser recording, API,
- status,
- date range,
- starred recordings,
- metadata fields when available.

Metadata filters are especially useful for contact-center workflows where calls include fields such as operator, phone number, team, or campaign.

## Reports

The **Reports** page shows:

- number of transcriptions,
- total duration,
- average duration,
- estimated time saved,
- storage used,
- source breakdown.

<Info>
  Time saved is an estimate based on the assumption that manual transcription takes about 5x the audio duration.
</Info>
