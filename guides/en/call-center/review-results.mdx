The checklist results page shows recordings analyzed with the selected QA checklist.

<Tip>
  This page is most useful for team leads who need to find low-scored calls, compare operators, or choose coaching examples.
</Tip>

## Table columns

The table shows recording information, status, creation time, analysis time, and checklist answers. Each checklist question becomes a separate column.

## Filters

Use search, status filters, and metadata filters to find important calls quickly. For example, you can show only failed or low-scored calls for a specific operator.

## Reading results

A question result can be:

- yes,
- no,
- partially,
- cannot determine,
- or a 1-10 score.

AI comments explain why the answer was assigned.

## Actions

- open the source recording,
- star important calls,
- edit the checklist,
- upload more calls,
- export results if export is enabled for your account.
