Bulk upload is for cases where you need to upload several calls under the same QA checklist.

<Info>
  Use bulk upload when you want to analyze a batch of calls without setting up an automated integration first.
</Info>

## When to use it

- reviewing a daily or weekly batch of calls,
- comparing operator performance,
- creating reports that can be filtered by metadata.

## Upload calls

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open AI Analysis">
    Select the checklist where results should appear.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add audio files">
    Upload one or more call recordings.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add metadata">
    Add fields such as operator, team, phone number, campaign, or call type.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start upload">
    Files move through upload, transcription, and analysis.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Metadata

Metadata helps you filter results later. Example fields:

- `operator`
- `team`
- `phone_number`
- `campaign`
- `call_type`

Use consistent key names across uploads so filters remain useful.

## Statuses

Files move through upload, transcription, and AI analysis. Completed results appear in the selected checklist’s results table.
