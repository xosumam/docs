A checklist is a set of questions Xosum.am uses to evaluate a recording.

<Tip>
  Checklists are useful when every call should be evaluated against the same criteria, regardless of who reviews the result manually.
</Tip>

## Create a checklist

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open AI Analysis">
    This is the Business-plan area for quality control.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create a checklist">
    Add a name and a short description.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add context">
    Include your rules, scripts, and scoring criteria.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add questions">
    Choose a question type and write one clear, testable question.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Context

Context helps AI understand your business rules. Add your sales script, required disclosures, allowed phrases, scoring criteria, or process rules.

## Question types

- **Yes / No** — when the answer can only be yes or no.
- **Yes / No / Partially** — when partial completion is possible.
- **Grade 1-10** — when you need a quality score.

## Good question examples

- “Did the operator introduce themselves at the beginning of the call?”
- “Did the operator explain the next steps?”
- “How clear was the operator’s speech on a scale of 1-10?”

<Warning>
  Avoid long, multi-part, or leading questions. One checklist question should test one specific thing.
</Warning>
